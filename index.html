Why Beyond Beach

We're not a package holiday.
We never were.

01
Small team, big care
We're a small, passionate crew based in South Devon. Most of us have actually been on these holidays — some of us met on them. That shows in everything we do, from the resorts we choose to the people we hire.
02
Active but unstructured
This isn't a reps-and-timetable holiday. Watersports, bikes, SUPs and sailing are all there when you want them — and so is a quiet spot on the beach when you don't. You decide what kind of day it's going to be.
03
Places with real character
We deliberately avoid the obvious. Kefalonia, Evia, Cabedelo — these are places with soul, not just sun. You won't bump into a thousand other tourists doing the same thing. That's the point.
04
Guests who keep coming back
We have families who've been coming for 15 years. Groups of friends who book the same week every summer. Children who grew up here and now bring their own kids. That's not marketing — that's the honest truth.
