Overview

Lixouri Bay Beach Club is our flagship resort on the beautiful Paliki peninsula in Kefalonia. Sitting right on a long sandy beach, it's the perfect base for families, couples and groups of friends who want an active holiday without the structure.

Your days here are entirely your own. The watersport equipment — kayaks, SUPs, dinghies, windsurf boards — is all there waiting on the beach, free to use whenever you like. Prefer dry land? Grab a mountain bike, join a yoga session, or try our BlitzFitness bootcamp on the sand. Or just find a sunlounger and do absolutely nothing. That works too.

The hotel has a pool, a beachfront restaurant, and a bar for evenings. The town of Lixouri is a 10-minute walk along the seafront — it's a real Greek town, not a tourist strip, with bakeries, tavernas and a harbour that comes alive at night.

Frequently asked questions

What's the flight time to Kefalonia?
Direct flights from the UK take around 3 hours 15 minutes. We fly from most major airports including Gatwick, Manchester, Birmingham and Bristol.
Is it suitable for young children?
Absolutely. The beach is sandy and shelves gently. We have equipment suitable for all ages and our team are experienced with families. Most of our guests come with children aged 4–16.
Do I need to book activities in advance?
No — everything is available on a turn-up-and-go basis. The kayaks, SUPs and bikes are right there on the beach. Yoga and BlitzFitness sessions run to a weekly timetable posted at the hotel.
What about food and drink?
Breakfast is included. Lunch and dinner are available at the hotel restaurant, or you can walk into Lixouri for authentic Greek tavernas. Most guests mix both throughout the week.
Can I add sailing to my beach club holiday?
Yes — our Intro to Sailing course runs from the same area. You can combine a beach club week with a sailing week for a two-week holiday. Talk to us for package pricing.
